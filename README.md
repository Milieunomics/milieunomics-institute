# Milieunomics Institute

Milieunomics Institute is an independent research initiative exploring the intersection of economics, environmental systems, and institutional design.

## Mission
To develop open knowledge, analytical tools, and research that promote sustainable systems, resilient societies, and transparent governance.

## Focus Areas
- Economic systems & institutional analysis
- Environmental & sustainability research
- Data-driven public-interest tools
- Open science & knowledge

## Principles
- Open access
- Transparency
- Interdisciplinary research
- Public-impact innovation

## Get Involved
Contributions, collaborations, and research partnerships are welcome.
